# Grove Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Grove ("the app") handles information.

## Summary

- Grove does not require an account.
- Grove does not include third-party analytics or ad tracking.
- Your notes and data are stored locally on your device by default.
- If you enable optional sync features, data may be synced through Apple iCloud/CloudKit under your Apple account.
- If you configure an external AI provider, content you submit to AI features is sent to that provider to generate responses.

## Data We Process

Grove can process the following categories of data that you create inside the app:

- Notes, boards, tags, connections, and related content
- App preferences and settings
- Optional URLs and metadata associated with saved items

## How Data Is Stored

- Local storage: Grove stores app data locally on your Mac by default.
- Optional cloud sync: If enabled by you, Grove may use Apple iCloud/CloudKit for synchronization.

## AI Features

Grove includes optional AI-assisted features. If you enable and use these features:

- Your prompt/context may be sent to the configured AI provider.
- Provider processing is subject to that provider's terms and privacy policy.
- You are responsible for reviewing and selecting your provider configuration.

## Tracking and Advertising

- Grove does not use advertising SDKs.
- Grove does not track you across apps or websites.

## Data Sharing

Grove does not sell personal data.

Data may be shared only when required to deliver features you explicitly use, such as:

- Apple iCloud/CloudKit (if sync is enabled)
- Configured AI provider endpoints (if AI features are used)

## Your Choices

You can:

- Use Grove fully in local-only mode
- Disable sync features
- Disable AI features or avoid submitting sensitive content
- Delete app data from your device

## Children's Privacy

Grove is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated over time. Material updates will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
